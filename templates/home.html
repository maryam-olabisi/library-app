You're Welcome To The University Library.

Mission




To become a world-class and reference Resource Centre that will enhance teaching, learning and research that will promote raising a new generation of scholars.

Vision




To build, conserve and preserve diverse information resources to support teaching, learning and research. Also, to provide life-long learning skills to our users.

The Library's aim is to provide good, aesthetic, user-friendly learning environment for all academic and research activities in the university.

Library Goals




To provide knowledge for all services of humanity


To meet the best global practices in librarianship


To promote advancement of learning and quality teaching


To institutionalize quality research


To provide good automation of library materials


To foster good partnership with other libraries


To provide instructional materials and other facilities for the pursuit of learning in all the programmes offerred in the University

Library Use

The following are, but not limited to, the rules to use the University Library. All Library users (Staff and Students) are to adhere to these rules.

More Library Rules


REGISTRATION

Registration should be done to become a Library member prior to using the Library resources.

LIBRARY CARD

Always come with your Library Card. Members must show their Library cards on entering the Library, when borrowing books, and at other times if asked to do so by the Porter, Security personnel or any member of the senior staff of the University Library.

COMPLETE SILENCE

Complete silence should be observed except for brief and subdued talk with the Library staff. No discussion is permitted inside the Library. Noise distracts other users.

NO AUDIO DEVICES

Electronic devices should be turned off. The use of Mobile phones or other audio instruments is strictly prohibited in the Library premises unless it is put in vibration or silent mode.

NO SMOKING

No smoking in or around the Library. The use of any form of candles, matches, hurricane lanterns and chemicals is prohibited in the Library.

BORROWER'S CARD

Library borrower cards are not transferable. The borrower is responsible for the books borrowed on his/her card.


BORROWED BOOKS

All books borrowed from the University Library should be returned before the reader goes on vacation.

LOG IN

Log In is required to keep track of users and resources

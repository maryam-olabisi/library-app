Library Use

The following are, but not limited to, the rules to use the University Library. All Library users (Staff and Students) are to adhere to these rules.


LIBRARY CARD

Always come with your Library Card. This is important to gain access to the Library Resources.

SILENCE

Users should maintain perfect silence. Electronic devices should be turned off. Noise distracts other users.

LOG IN

Log In is required to keep track of users and resources
